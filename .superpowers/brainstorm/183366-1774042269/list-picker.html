Picking a shopping list

After tapping "Add X items to list", how should you pick which shopping list to add to?

A

Bottom sheet drawer

Slides up from the bottom (like AddItemDrawer / ManageUsersDrawer). Lists your shopping lists, tap one to confirm and add.

B

Inline in select mode

The list picker appears directly on the recipe page above the CTA button — no drawer needed. Select a list then tap Add.
